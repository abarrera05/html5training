Specificity: The less than obvious

The * selector, or global selector, has no value.

* {} 0-0-0

Combinators, like ~, >, and + have no value

ul li {} 0-0-2
ul > li {} 0-0-2

:not has no value, but parameter selector does

.myClass:not(p) {} 0-1-1
